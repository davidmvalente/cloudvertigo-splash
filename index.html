  • 1. Challenge the status quo

    Foster the strongest contrarian opinions, be ready and willing to ask those hard “What If?” questions.

  • 2. Re-frame understanding with analogies

    Play with different assumptions sets and look at the fundamental structure of things without fear, blame or judgment.

    Everything we know exists in relationship. We are little more than the stories we tell ourselves. We can choose which stories to tend to with our hearts and minds. Fiction is another word for freedom.
